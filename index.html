Welcome

TBR is a book club dedicated to giving its members a chance to read a good (or bad) book.

Too often the excuse, there is never enough time destroys a would-be reader!

TBR is here to remind members to read through social obligations!

However, it is never necessary to start or finish any book. You are always welcome at TBR meetings!
